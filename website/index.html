Why choose Shotera?

Blazing Fast

Written in Rust, Shotera uses minimal system resources and starts instantly, capturing your screen with zero lag.

100% Offline & Private

No telemetry, no tracking. All captures and edits happen completely offline on your own device.

Pin to Screen

Pin important screenshots above all other windows. Perfect for reference while coding or designing.

Powerful Editor

Instantly draw arrows, boxes, text, or blur sensitive information before saving or copying.

Quick Cloud Upload

Upload screenshots to Imgur with a single click and instantly copy the link to your clipboard.

Text Recognition (OCR)

Extract text from uncopyable areas on your screen with a single click and copy it as text.

Step Counter

Click on the screen to add auto-incrementing numbers (1, 2, 3). Perfect for tutorials.

Multi-Format Support

Save your screenshots in PNG, JPG, or modern WebP formats with adjustable quality settings.

Designed to be elegant

Shotera provides a modern, customizable Settings interface available in both English and Turkish. Define your own global shortcuts to trigger area or fullscreen captures natively.

  • ✓ Custom Global Shortcuts (e.g., Cmd+Shift+S)
  • ✓ Multi-format support (PNG, JPG, WebP)
  • ✓ Run in background / system tray
